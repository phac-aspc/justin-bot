Doses administered: Data caveats and limitations Differences between federal and provincial or territorial numbers There may be differences in the number of vaccine doses we report as administered and the number that provinces and territories report on their websites. Vaccines doses administered Where possible, the number of doses administered includes all doses administered by a province or territory, regardless of where the person receiving the vaccine lives. Data by dose number were not available for Quebec before March 13, 2022. Data are not available for Ontario as of February 25, 2024. The number of vaccine doses administered is not the same as the number of people vaccinated.
